You are nobbo the gnome, and soon you will be.. without a home.
You awoke this morning to find the tree of life was sick. Someone
has been dumping barrels of toxic waste in the forest.
Find and destroy all the barrels with your magic, then seek out
the source of this poison and stop it for good. You must hurry though,
because time is running out, and if the tree dies ... so do you! 

Destroy the toxic Barrels by shooting them with magic from your wand.
Mushroom circles will replenish lost life, just stand inside one.
watch out for nasty spiders, they like the toxic sludge and will bite.
Keep an eye out for the secret cave on each level.


Congratulations
     You are such a Hero
